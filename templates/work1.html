001 : Deals on Wheels

User can rent as well as provide their vehicle via Android app. This app has multiple functionalities like Google Authentication, GPS Tracking, Cloud storage, Real-Time Data Communication, Customer Service, Digi-Card, Mathematical and Statistical Reasoning for calculating Vehicle Potential, Graphical representation for Monthly Business and Machine Learning for Car Price prediction with approximately 80% accuracy. Also, Admin can view and manage all activities such as viewing all cars, all users and also handling issues asked in customer service section.
